Our Approach

Five calm steps. No hard sell.

You wouldn't let a stranger build your roof. We don't think you should let one build your energy system either.

01 · Listen

Free Energy Audit

We assess your power bill with your current situation, future requirements and budget. No pressure — we just listen to how you use power.

02 · Design

Home Assessment, Roof Type, Electrical Audit, Design Recommended System

Tailored to your needs with finance options explained. It's our mission to be clear on each step and the processes we take to assist you.

03 · Approve

Grid & Rebate Paperwork

SAPN application, STC paperwork, CHB rebate — we lodge it all so you don't have to chase a single form.

04 · Install

Installation Day

Our project manager (Caleb or Troy) meets the install crew in the morning to walk through job requirements. A safety pre-start runs first and a safe zone is established, with constant communication kept with the client throughout. Brief power outages may be required where necessary.

05 · Live

Commissioning & Care

System tested, app paired. From our VPP customers to our regular customers, full technical support is on hand — we're a phone call away.

Government Rebates · 2026

What South Australia takes off the price.

You don't lodge anything — we apply every eligible rebate straight off your invoice. The prices on our packages already reflect what you'll actually pay.

Auto-applied
Federal · Solar

STC Rebate

Small-scale Technology Certificates — we register your install with the Clean Energy Regulator and discount the certificate value off your invoice. Currently ~$400/kW for SA Zone 3, 5-year deeming.

Auto-applied
Federal · Battery

Cheaper Home Batteries

The new federal battery rebate — ~$315/kWh indicative on eligible battery systems. Applied straight to your quote. Available on systems installed from 1 July 2025 onward.

Optional
SA Power Networks

Virtual Power Plant Bonus

Opt-in for the SAPN VPP program with a compatible battery and earn additional annual credits on top of your bill savings. Ask us which inverter / battery combos qualify.

Rebate values are indicative for residential installs in SA Zone 3, 2026. Final discount confirmed on quote.
